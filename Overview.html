Overview

SRINIVASARAO MODEL HIGH SCHOOL was established in the year 2001 with a motto of providing quality education in a highly disciplined environment with standards along with character building of students, who will be able to stand up to the challenges of present employment.We strive to provide an academically challenging environment to increase intellectual curiosity and a sense of responsibility in our students.A happy and respectful rapport between children and staff at SRMHS is a wonderful platform for engaged learning. Small class sizes ensure children can work at their own pace while being encouraged, challenged, and rewarded in all aspects of their school life.

Mission

Our mission is to build a strong foundation for future generations. Our students are encouraged to build their personalities by participating in several co-curricular activities like debate, quizzes, essay writing, painting, etc. They are allowed to participate in seminars and cultural functions.

Objective

To inculcate our traditional values, and modern educational concepts in kids and try to build self-confidence and independence in children.

Vision

Apart from acquiring excellence and distinction in academics, students should be inspired to become good citizens, and imbibe patriotism towards the motherland and a sense of respect for the elders
